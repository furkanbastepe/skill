description Operational Briefing

1

Target Acquisition

Locate the Blue Artifact within the randomly generated maze. The artifact's coordinates are provided at runtime via the `get_target_pos()` method.

2

Obstacle Avoidance

Static sentries (Red Zones) deal 25 damage upon contact. Your unit has 100 HP. Pathfinding algorithms like A* or Dijkstra are highly recommended to minimize damage.

3

Extraction

Once the artifact is secured, return to the starting zone (Green) to complete the mission. Efficiency is key; completing the mission in fewer ticks yields a higher score multiplier.
